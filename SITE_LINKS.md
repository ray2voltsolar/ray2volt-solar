# Ray2Volt Solar — Site Pages

A list of every page in this project with a short description. Paths are relative to the project root.

## Main Navigation

- **[index.html](index.html)** — Home page with hero, stats, services overview, and "Why Choose Us" section.
  Entry point linking to Residential, Commercial, Investor, and About pages.

- **[about.html](about.html)** — Company overview page covering Ray2Volt's mission, vision, and background.
  Serves as the "About Us" link in the main navigation.

- **[residential.html](residential.html)** — Residential solar hub page summarizing On-Grid and Hybrid options.
  Links onward to the two dedicated residential product pages below.

  - **[residential-ongrid.html](residential-ongrid.html)** — Details On-Grid residential solar systems and bill savings.
    Includes PM Surya Ghar subsidy information and hero stats.

  - **[residential-hybrid.html](residential-hybrid.html)** — Details Hybrid residential solar with battery backup.
    Emphasizes uninterrupted power supply and energy independence.

- **[commercial.html](commercial.html)** — Commercial & Industrial solar hub with hero, CAPEX/OPEX models, and industries grid.
  Links out to all eight dedicated industry pages below.

- **[investor.html](investor.html)** — RESCO investment model page for people wanting to fund solar projects.
  Explains the returns model and links to downloadable investor material.

- **[contact.html](contact.html)** — Contact page with address, phone, email, and a lead-capture contact form.
  Includes an embedded map and WhatsApp contact option.

## Industry Pages (linked from Commercial)

- **[industry-manufacturing.html](industry-manufacturing.html)** — Solar for factories and production units.
  Focuses on daytime load matching and accelerated depreciation benefits.

- **[industry-hotels.html](industry-hotels.html)** — Solar for hotels, resorts, and hospitality businesses.
  Highlights round-the-clock energy demand and green-brand appeal.

- **[industry-retail.html](industry-retail.html)** — Solar for retail stores, supermarkets, and malls.
  Covers business-hours load matching and solar carport options.

- **[industry-healthcare.html](industry-healthcare.html)** — Solar for hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic centres.
  Emphasizes reliable power for facilities that can never lose power.

- **[industry-education.html](industry-education.html)** — Solar for schools, colleges, and universities.
  Covers large campus rooftops and using generation data as a teaching tool.

- **[industry-offices.html](industry-offices.html)** — Solar for corporate offices and IT parks.
  Focuses on ESG goals, ESG reporting, and green building certification.

- **[industry-cold-storage.html](industry-cold-storage.html)** — Solar for cold storage and food processing facilities.
  Explains why peak cooling demand matches peak solar output.

- **[industry-warehousing.html](industry-warehousing.html)** — Solar for warehouses and logistics hubs.
  Covers monetizing large unused rooftops and powering future EV charging.

## Lead-Generation & Landing Pages

- **[get-quote.html](get-quote.html)** — Standalone quote-request form for prospective customers.
  Captures contact details and routes submissions through Web3Forms.

- **[claim-subsidy.html](claim-subsidy.html)** — Ad landing page focused on the ₹78,000 PM Surya Ghar subsidy.
  Designed to drive subsidy-specific lead form submissions.

- **[claim-discount.html](claim-discount.html)** — Ad landing page combining the subsidy with a ₹24,000 seasonal discount.
  Highlights a combined ₹1,02,000 savings offer with a lead form.

- **[save-on-solar.html](save-on-solar.html)** — Alternate ad landing page bundling subsidy and discount messaging.
  Near-duplicate of claim-discount/claim-subsidy for campaign testing.

- **[solar-in-tirupati.html](solar-in-tirupati.html)** — Location-specific SEO landing page targeting "Tirupati" searches.
  Includes local trust signals like installation counts and free power audits.

- **[thank-you.html](thank-you.html)** — Post-submission confirmation page shown after a form is completed.
  Confirms receipt and sets expectations for next steps.

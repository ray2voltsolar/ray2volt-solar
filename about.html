About Ray2Volt Solar

Empowering communities with clean, affordable solar energy since our founding.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of solar energy in India by making it accessible, affordable, and hassle-free for every household and business.

At Ray2Volt Solar, we believe that clean energy is not just for the privileged few – it's a right for everyone. Our mission drives us to work tirelessly, bringing world-class solar technology to homes and businesses across India.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted solar energy partner in Andhra Pradesh, known for our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and community impact. We envision a future where every rooftop harnesses the power of the sun.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Ray2Volt Solar.

Integrity

We promise only what we can deliver and always stand by our commitments. Honest advice, transparent pricing, no hidden costs.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our success. We go the extra mile to ensure every customer is happy with their solar installation.

Quality Excellence

We never compromise on quality. From Tier-1 panels to expert installation, we ensure top-notch standards in everything.

Proudly Local

We understand the unique needs of our customers.

Being a local company means we're always accessible. When you need support, you don't have to call a distant call center – you reach real people who know your area and care about your experience.

  • Quick Response Time – We can be at your doorstep within hours, not days.

  • Local Knowledge – We understand local grid conditions, weather patterns, and regulations.

  • Community Commitment – Your success is our success. We're invested in our community's future.

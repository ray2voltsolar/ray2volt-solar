Flexible Ownership Models

Choose the model that works best for your business.

CAPEX Model

Own your solar system outright.

  • Strong long-term savings potential

  • Accelerated depreciation benefits

  • Full ownership of the asset

  • May qualify for applicable government incentives

Best for: Businesses with capital available for investment and seeking strong long-term ROI.

OPEX / PPA Model

Zero upfront investment. Pay only for power consumed.

  • No capital investment required

  • Savings begin once your PPA rate is confirmed

  • No maintenance hassles - we handle it

  • Fixed power rate for contract duration

Best for: Businesses wanting to save without capital investment.

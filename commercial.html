Flexible Ownership Models

Choose the model that works best for your business.

CAPEX Model

Own your solar system outright.

  • Potential for stronger long-term savings through asset ownership

  • Potential tax treatment to review with your tax adviser

  • Full ownership of the asset

  • Net-metering and DISCOM coordination support, subject to applicable rules

May suit: Businesses able to fund the project and own the solar asset.

OPEX / PPA Model

A third-party funded power-purchase model, where project eligibility and commercial terms permit.

  • May avoid upfront project capital when an eligible investor approves the project

  • Proposed energy rate and savings are confirmed after commercial evaluation

  • Operations and maintenance responsibilities are defined in the final agreement

  • Tariff and escalation terms are defined in the executed PPA

May suit: Qualifying projects that meet investor, site, load and credit criteria.

Ownership model availability, savings, payback, tax treatment, approvals and component warranties are project-specific and will be stated in the site-based proposal and final contract.

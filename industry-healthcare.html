Daytime Solar for High-Load Healthcare

Hospitals run 24x7 with heavy HVAC, imaging, and life-support loads - and electricity is a cost that never sleeps.

Healthcare facilities are among the most energy-intensive buildings per square metre. Operating theatres, diagnostic imaging, sterilisation, and round-the-clock climate control create massive, non-negotiable demand.

On-grid solar can offset part of the daytime load while the grid is available. It works alongside existing backup systems but does not replace UPS, generator or other critical-power arrangements.

  • Offset heavy daytime HVAC and equipment loads

  • Evaluate potential electricity savings alongside patient-care budgets

  • Integrate daytime solar without replacing required clinical backup systems

  • Meet green-building norms for healthcare campuses

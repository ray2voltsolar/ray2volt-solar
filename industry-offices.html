Solar for Corporate Offices

Lower OPEX, stronger ESG scores, and greener certified workspaces.

Power Your Workplace with Sunshine

Offices run lighting, computing, and HVAC through standard working hours - the heart of the solar generation window.

Corporate buildings and IT parks consume almost all their energy between 9 AM and 6 PM. Rooftop solar serves that load directly, cutting common-area and tenant electricity costs while insulating the facility from tariff inflation.

For companies with ESG commitments, on-site solar is one of the most visible and measurable steps toward net-zero targets - and it supports green building certifications like IGBC and LEED.

  • Office-hours load aligns exactly with generation

  • Measurable progress on ESG and net-zero goals

  • Supports IGBC / LEED green building certification

  • Lower common-area maintenance costs for tenants

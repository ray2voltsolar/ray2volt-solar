Solar for Retail & Malls

Long daytime operating hours make retail one of the best matches for solar power.

Brighter Stores, Lower Bills

Lighting, air conditioning, and refrigeration run from morning to night - the exact window when solar generates.

Retail spaces and malls consume heavily during business hours: storefront lighting, HVAC for shopper comfort, escalators, and cold chains for grocery. Rooftop and carport solar can offset a large share of this load at a locked-in cost.

Malls with large terraces and parking areas can go further with solar carports - generating power while giving customers shaded parking.

  • Solar carports add generation plus shaded parking

  • Protect thin retail margins from tariff hikes

  • Visible sustainability that shoppers and tenants value

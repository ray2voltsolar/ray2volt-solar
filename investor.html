What is the RESCO Model?

RESCO (Renewable Energy Service Company) is an investment model where you fund solar projects and earn returns from the electricity generated.

Here's how it works: You invest in a solar power plant installed on a commercial/industrial rooftop. The electricity generated is sold to the host (building owner) at a fixed rate. You receive your share of the revenue as regular payments.

Key Benefits:

  • Predictable Returns – Fixed power purchase agreements ensure stable revenue

  • No Technical Hassle – We handle installation, maintenance, and operations

  • Long-term Asset – Solar panels have 25+ year lifespan

  • Green Investment – Contribute to sustainability and reduce carbon footprint

What is a Hybrid System?

A hybrid solar system combines grid connectivity with lithium-ion battery storage. It generates solar power during the day, stores excess energy in batteries, and seamlessly switches to backup during power cuts.

Unlike on-grid systems that shut down during outages, hybrid systems keep your essential appliances running 24/7. You still enjoy net metering benefits while having complete peace of mind.

  • Uninterrupted power during grid outages

  • Store excess solar energy for nighttime use

  • Maximum energy independence from the grid

  • Still eligible for net metering credits

Ideal for: Areas with frequent power cuts or homes requiring uninterrupted power for ACs, WiFi, medical equipment, etc.

How Hybrid Solar Works

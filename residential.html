Solar Solutions for Your Home

Power your home with clean energy. Reduce bills, gain independence, and contribute to a greener future.

Choose Your System Type

We offer two main types of solar systems to match your needs and budget.

On-Grid Solar System

Connected to the electricity grid. Most economical option.

  • Lower upfront cost – no battery required

  • Export excess power to grid and earn credits

  • Eligible for PM Surya Ghar subsidy

  • Minimal maintenance

Note: Does not provide backup during power cuts as it requires grid connection to operate.

Hybrid Solar System

Grid-connected with battery backup. Best of both worlds.

  • Power backup during outages

  • Store excess energy for night use

  • Maximum energy independence

  • Still eligible for net metering benefits

Ideal for: Areas with frequent power cuts or homes requiring uninterrupted power.

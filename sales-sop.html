Sales SOP & Technical Details

Standard Operating Procedures and Technical guides for the Ray2Volt Sales Team. Use this to educate customers and generate accurate Bill of Materials.

1. On-Grid Solar Working Demo

An On-Grid solar system works in tandem with the utility grid. Use this interactive power flow schematic to show customers how energy is generated and distributed during the day and night.

Day Mode Night Mode

☀️ Day Mode Operation

  • Generation: Solar panels generate DC electricity from sunlight.
  • Conversion: The solar inverter converts DC to usable AC power.
  • Consumption: AC power directly feeds the Home Load.
  • Export: Any excess power generated is exported to the Utility Grid through the Net Meter, earning credits.

🌙 Night Mode Operation

  • No Solar: Solar panels are inactive without sunlight.
  • Grid Import: Electricity is seamlessly imported from the Utility Grid to power the Home Load.
  • Net Metering: The Net Meter records the imported energy, which is offset against the daytime export credits.
